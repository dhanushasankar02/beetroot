At Beetroot Detox, we believe that the earth provides everything we need to thrive. Our mission is to bottle that wisdom.

Our Story

Founded in 2012 by a team of nutritionists and farmers, Beetroot Detox was born out of a simple realization: the modern diet was missing the raw, vibrant energy of the earth.

We spent years researching the unique properties of beetroots, carrots, and medicinal herbs, developing a cold-press process that preserves every enzyme and antioxidant.

Today, we partner with 40+ organic farms to bring you juices that aren't just beverages, but liquid life force.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make, from seed to bottle.

Purity First

No added sugars, no preservatives, and no artificial flavors. Just pure, cold-pressed plants.

Sustainability

We use 100% recyclable glass and support regenerative farming practices to protect our planet.

Transparency

Know exactly where your juice came from. We trace every bottle back to the specific farm plot.

Ethical Sourcing

We ensure fair wages and long-term contracts for our 40+ partner farmers, fostering a resilient local economy.

Holistic Wellness

We see juice as a catalyst for a healthier lifestyle, offering education and support beyond the bottle.

Community First

We actively give back to local food banks and wellness programs, ensuring healthy nutrition is accessible to all.

The Journey to Your Door

Sustainable Harvest

Our farmers hand-pick produce at the peak of ripeness, ensuring maximum nutrient density.

Cold-Press Extraction

We use hydraulic pressure to extract juice without heat, preserving vital enzymes and vitamins.

Antioxidant Protection

Bottling happens in a chilled environment to prevent oxidation and lock in freshness.

Carbon-Neutral Delivery

Direct from our hubs to your home, using optimized routes to minimize our footprint.

The Science of Bioavailability

Our "Innovation Lab" isn't just about mixing flavors. It's where we study how your body absorbs nutrients. By combining beetroot with specific botanical catalysts like ginger and lemon, we increase the absorption rate of nitrates and Vitamin C by up to 30%.

Lab Verified

Every batch undergoes enzyme activity testing to ensure the "live" qualities of our cold-press process remain intact from our facility to your fridge.

The Zero-Waste Vision

Our commitment to the Earth goes beyond our ingredients. We've built a circular system that honors every part of the root.

Composted Pulp

100% of our leftover fibrous pulp is returned to our partner farms as nutrient-dense organic compost.

Water Recovery

Our facility uses advanced filtration to recycle 60% of water used in the cleaning process for irrigation.

Plastic-Free Shipping

From corn-starch insulation to recycled paper tape, your delivery is 100% biodegradable.

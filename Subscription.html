Subscription Intelligence

How are the juices kept cold during delivery?
We use custom-engineered, biodegradable insulation and non-toxic ice packs that maintain a temperature of 2-4°C for up to 12 hours at your doorstep.
Can I customize my box every week?
Absolutely. You have until 48 hours before your delivery window to swap flavors in your dashboard. If you don't choose, we'll send our "Farmer's Pick" seasonal rotation.
What is the glass return program?
For "The Alchemist" tier, leave your clean empty bottles in the delivery bag. We collect, sanitize, and reuse them — reducing our carbon footprint by 40% compared to recycling.

Beetroot & Juice
Nutrition Guide

Unlock nature’s ruby root — packed with nitrates, antioxidants, and vibrant energy. Discover why this crimson superfood transforms wellness from the inside out.

01. Nutritional Powerhouse

Beetroot is low in calories yet packed with essential vitamins and minerals. A single cup delivers folate, manganese, potassium, iron, and vitamin C — each playing a vital role in metabolism and immunity.

  • Folate (B9): Supports cell function & tissue growth
  • Manganese: Bone health & antioxidant defense
  • Potassium: Heart rhythm & muscle contractions
  • Dietary fiber: Aids digestion & gut health

02. Remarkable Health Benefits

Regular consumption of beetroot and its juice has been linked to lower blood pressure, enhanced athletic endurance, and reduced inflammation. The natural nitrates convert to Nitric Oxide, widening blood vessels.

  • Blood pressure regulation: Natural vasodilation effect
  • Exercise performance: Improves oxygen efficiency + delays fatigue
  • Anti-inflammatory: Betalains fight oxidative stress
  • Brain health: Increased blood flow to frontal lobe

03. How to Make Beetroot Juice

Unlock maximum flavor & nutrients in three simple methods. Always choose organic beets for the best taste and health benefits. Combine with complementary fruits to balance earthiness.

  • Juicer method: Wash, peel (optional), juice raw beets with ginger
  • Blender method: Blend chopped beets + water, strain through nut milk bag
  • Pro tip: Add lemon, apple or carrot to cut earthy tones
  • Storage: Drink fresh within 24h for maximum nutrient retention

04. Optimal Timing & Dosage

When and how much you consume impacts results. Start with small amounts to allow your body to adjust. Pre-workout or morning consumption offers the biggest benefits.

  • Daily recommendation: 150–250ml (5–8 oz) of pure juice
  • Best time to drink: 30–60 minutes before exercise or on empty stomach
  • Athletes: 2–3 hours before endurance training
  • Caution: Beeturia (red urine) is harmless, but monitor if prone to oxalates

05. Delicious Beetroot Juice Recipes

Make your daily beetroot ritual irresistible with creative combinations. These recipes balance sweetness, tang, and earthiness while multiplying nutrients.

  • Ruby Stamina: 1 beetroot + 2 carrots + 1 orange + ½ lemon
  • Golden Glow: 1 beet + 1 apple + 1-inch ginger + cucumber
  • Berry Beet Blast: ½ beet + handful of berries + splash of coconut water
  • Spicy Detox: Beet + celery + turmeric + black pepper (enhances curcumin absorption)

06. Selection & Storage Mastery

Peak flavor starts with choosing the right beets and keeping them fresh. Learn how to preserve nutrients and avoid waste whether you juice immediately or store for later.

  • Selection: Firm, smooth, deep red/purple beets with crisp greens (no wilting)
  • Storage (fresh roots): Remove greens, store in perforated bag in fridge for 2 weeks
  • Freezing: Blanch chopped beets; freeze for up to 6 months
  • Juice prep ahead: Store in airtight glass jar (fridge) max 24–48h

07. Beetroot for Detox & Skin Glow

Deep red pigments (betalains) assist liver detoxification pathways and combat oxidative stress. Beetroot juice nourishes skin from within — reducing inflammation, dark spots, and promoting a natural flush.

  • Liver support: Betalains stimulate phase II detox enzymes
  • Skin clarity: Vitamin C & antioxidants boost collagen, fight acne
  • Natural detox juice: Blend beet + lemon + mint + cucumber for morning cleanse
  • Blood purification: Iron & folate enhance red blood cell production → rosy complexion
